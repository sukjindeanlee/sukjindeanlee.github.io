Dean Lee's Interpreted Foods

1. Stir Fry Noodles-

Easy

- Ramen Noodles
- Tofu
- Green Onion
- Red Chili
- Garlic
- Ginger
- Soy Sauce
- Oyster Sauce

Instructions: Pat dry a half a block of Tofu and crush it with a fork.
Add Tofu to a hot oiled pan.
Add sliced Green Onion, Red Chili, Garlic, and Ginger.
Add a drop of Soy Sauce and stir.
Follow up with a decent amount of Oyster Sauce along with Water.
Add cooked Ramen Noodles and stir it.
