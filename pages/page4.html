Prison Break

This show was one of the first shows I truly was invested in after covid-19. From its diverse characters to the intricate plots that have the audience wanting more, it's truly an amazing show about two brothers who seem to have no way out of an impossible situation to make it in an intense and wild prison. Overall, the actions and plot really made this show amazing.

Suits

Suits is one of those shows that have you thinking about how your professional worklife can ultimately affect your personal life. There are so many different cases that the two main characters tackle that create suspense and excitement at the same time. I throughly enjoyed watching Mike Ross and Harvey Specter work together.

Breaking Bad

Breaking Bad is a show I'm currently watching. It's plot is definitely engaging as a high school chemistry teacher starts to find different ways of obtaining money after seeing everything and everyone fall apart. I loved the cast for the show and even Jesse Pinkman who works with Walter White to make it big. I recommend that people watch the show if they are big on action.
